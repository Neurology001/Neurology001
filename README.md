## Focus

I work at the intersection of neurology, clinical reasoning, and deployable medical software. The goal is simple: make clinical data easier to capture, models easier to inspect, and digital tools easier to trust.

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">

    </td>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">
      <h3>Clinical Prediction</h3>

    </td>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">

## Design Principle

> Clinical tools should feel calm, precise, and accountable. The interface should reduce cognitive load before it tries to impress anyone.

<br />

## Current Direction

- Turning clinical prediction research into usable, reviewable software.
- Building structured neurology registry workflows that can grow across disease areas.
- Refining medical AI outputs so they remain clinically interpretable, reproducible, and deployment-ready.
